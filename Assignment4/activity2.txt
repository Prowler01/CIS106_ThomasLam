This program takes an input user age and converts it into approximate age in months, days, hours, and seconds. 

Input:
    Display prompt
    Get user age

Process:
    Convert age in years to months
	Convert age in years to days
	Convert age in years to hours
	Convert age in years to seconds

Output:
    Display age in months
	Display age in days
	Display age in hours
	Display age in seconds
